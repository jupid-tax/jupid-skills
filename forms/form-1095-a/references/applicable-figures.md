# Applicable Figures (Form 8962 Line 7)

The Applicable Figure is the percentage of household income the user is expected to contribute toward Marketplace premiums. PTC fills the gap between this contribution and the SLCSP benchmark.

Two regimes:
1. **Pre-ARPA (TCJA-era, 2014–2020)**: applicable figures range from 2.0% (at 133% FPL) up to 9.86% (at 400% FPL); above 400% FPL → no PTC
2. **ARPA/IRA-extended (2021–2025)**: lower applicable figures across the board; above 400% FPL → still eligible if benchmark plan exceeds 8.5% of income

**As of April 2026, post-2025 status depends on legislation.** Verify Form 8962 instructions and Pub 974 for the year being filed.

---

## ARPA/IRA-extended Applicable Figures (2021–2025)

| Line 5 (% of FPL) | Applicable Figure |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| Less than 150% | 0.0000 (0%) |
| 150% | 0.0000 |
| 200% | 0.0200 (2.0%) |
| 250% | 0.0400 (4.0%) |
| 300% | 0.0600 (6.0%) |
| 400% | 0.0850 (8.5%) |
| Over 400% | 0.0850 (capped at 8.5%) |

Linear interpolation between brackets. Example: at 175% FPL, applicable figure = (175 − 150) / (200 − 150) × (0.02 − 0.00) = 0.0100 (1.0%).

## Pre-ARPA Applicable Figures (pre-2021, possibly resumes for 2026)

| Line 5 (% of FPL) | Applicable Figure |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| Less than 133% | 0.0204 (2.04%) |
| 133% | 0.0304 (3.04%) |
| 150% | 0.0405 (4.05%) |
| 200% | 0.0639 (6.39%) |
| 250% | 0.0817 (8.17%) |
| 300% | 0.0962 (9.62%) |
| 400% | 0.0986 (9.86%) |
| Over 400% | NO PTC ELIGIBILITY |

(Figures from Rev. Proc. 2020-36; verify exact percentages for the year being filed.)

The most consequential difference: pre-ARPA, anyone over 400% FPL got **zero** PTC, often called the "subsidy cliff." A taxpayer at 401% FPL could end up owing back the entire APTC. ARPA/IRA removed this cliff.

---

## How to look up

1. Determine Line 5 (Income % of FPL).
2. Find the row that matches or interpolate between rows.
3. For the ARPA/IRA-extended table, anything 150% and below → 0.0000 (no contribution required).
4. Enter the figure on Form 8962 Line 7.
